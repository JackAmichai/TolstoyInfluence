# Tolstoy Influence — Business Explainer Video

**Format:** Presenter-led explainer with B-roll + live product demo
**Target runtime:** ~2:30
**Tone:** Warm, conversational, confident. The presenter is a parent first, a pitch-person second.
**Logline:** A dad notices his teenager doesn't *shop* anymore — she *watches* — and realizes that's exactly the gap his business has been losing money to.

> Casting note: written as a teenage daughter for concreteness — swap to son / "my kid" freely.
> Lines marked **PRD quote** are pulled verbatim from `prd.html` and are intended as on-screen supers.

---

## SCENE 1 — COLD OPEN · "How my daughter shops"
**(0:00 – 0:25)**

**VISUAL:** Living room, natural light. A teenage girl on the couch, knees up, phone vertical, thumb flicking through a creator's try-on haul. We see her screen: an influencer in a camel blazer. She pauses, tilts her head, screenshots it. PRESENTER leans on the doorway, watching, half-amused.

**PRESENTER (to camera, low-key):**
"My daughter hasn't 'gone shopping' in about two years. But she buys more clothes than ever. She just does it like *this* —"

**VISUAL:** Push in on her screen — she's now in a different app, reverse-image-searching the blazer, three tabs deep, giving up.

**PRESENTER (V.O.):**
"She sees the look. She wants the look. And then she hits the wall everyone hits: *where do I actually buy this?*"

**SUPER (lower third):** *Tolstoy Influence*

---

## SCENE 2 — THE SHIFT · Market Need
**(0:25 – 1:00)**

**VISUAL:** Presenter now seated, talking to camera. Cut in fast B-roll: TikTok/Reels scroll, creators in outfits, "add to cart" never appearing.

**PRESENTER:**
"Here's the thing every retailer needs to sit with —"

**SUPER (PRD quote, type-on):**
> "Consumers no longer search for products — they discover them through creator content on TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube. Yet the infrastructure to connect 'what I see' to 'what I can buy' has not kept up."

**PRESENTER:**
"Brands already know this. So they spend *millions* gifting product to creators — thousands of videos of people wearing your stuff. And then?"

**VISUAL:** A beautiful creator video literally dissolving into a social feed and scrolling away into the void.

**PRESENTER (V.O.):**
"It lives and dies on a feed. It's never captured, never matched to your inventory, never made shoppable."

**SUPER (PRD quote):**
> "Brands pay for content that generates brand awareness but zero direct revenue attribution."

**PRESENTER (back to camera, leans in):**
"Because here's the gap nobody's closed —"

**SUPER (PRD quote, held):**
> "No platform today can look at a creator video, identify every garment, check real-time stock, and present a buy button — automatically."

**PRESENTER:**
"That's the gap. So we built the bridge."

---

## SCENE 3 — THE REVEAL · What it is
**(1:00 – 1:20)**

**VISUAL:** Hard cut to the Tolstoy Influence storefront (the **Shopper view**). The daughter's same camel-blazer video — but now it opens into a clean shoppable look: items, sizes, "Add Full Outfit."

**PRESENTER (V.O.):**
"This is **Tolstoy Influence** — the newest product in the Tolstoy suite, alongside Stories, Live, and Pages."

**SUPER (PRD quote):**
> "Turning organic creator videos into a structured, shoppable, and inventory-matched catalog."

**VISUAL:** Daughter taps a size, taps add — frictionless.

**PRESENTER (V.O.):**
"For your shopper, it's simple: no searching, no link-hunting — *watch, tap, and own the look.*"

---

## SCENE 4 — HOW IT WORKS · The 3 layers
**(1:20 – 2:00)**

**VISUAL:** Switch into the **Business / Brand view** of the demo. Three quick animated "layers" build on screen as the presenter counts them.

**PRESENTER (to camera):**
"And for *you* — the brand — it runs in three layers, automatically."

**LAYER 1 — SMART CURATION**
**VISUAL:** Feed of creator videos; non-fashion clips (vlogs, travel) greying out and dropping away.
**PRESENTER (V.O.):** "One — it finds the right creators and keeps only the apparel content. No vlogs, no noise."

**LAYER 2 — AI DETECTION & SKU MATCHING**
**VISUAL:** The modal scanning the blazer; detection boxes; the three match badges appear.
**PRESENTER (V.O.):** "Two — computer vision detects every garment and matches each one against your live catalog, three ways:"
**SUPER (animate in):**
- 🟢 **STRICT** — exact brand + category + colour + size
- 🟠 **SOFT** — brand + colour confirmed, size differs
- 🔴 **FAIL** — not in inventory → the video is excluded

**LAYER 3 — HUMAN-IN-THE-LOOP**
**VISUAL:** The Business review screen — items with **Approve / Decline**, the live "*2 of 4 approved — shoppers will see 2 items*" tally, then "Publish look."
**PRESENTER (V.O.):**
"Three — nothing goes live on autopilot."

**SUPER (PRD quote):**
> "Videos scoring above 70% confidence enter a review queue. Your sales or operations team reviews and approves them before they go live."

**PRESENTER (on camera, tapping Approve):**
"Your team has the final say on every single item — *is it in stock, do I want to sell it* — and only then does it reach a shopper."

**SUPER (PRD quote):**
> "Only videos where every visible product is in stock appear on the storefront."

## SCENE 5 — THE PAYOFF · Why it's worth it
**(2:00 – 2:25)**

**VISUAL:** Three clean stat cards snap in, one per beat.

**PRESENTER:**
"So what does that do for the business? Three things."

**CARD 1 — NEW CONVERSION CHANNEL**
**SUPER (PRD quote):** "Influencer content converts at 2–3× the rate of brand-produced content — authentic, contextual, and trusted by followers."
**PRESENTER (V.O.):** "It converts two to three times better than the content you make yourself."

**CARD 2 — ZERO-WASTE CONTENT ROI**
**SUPER (PRD quote):** "Instead of letting them expire on social feeds, Influence captures, matches, and monetizes them."
**PRESENTER (V.O.):** "The seeding budget you *already* spend starts generating direct, attributed revenue."

**CARD 3 — INVENTORY-SAFE COMMERCE**
**SUPER (PRD quote):** "Products that are out of stock never appear. Products in the wrong colour never appear. Only purchasable items are shown."
**PRESENTER (V.O.):** "And it kills the number-one trust killer in video commerce —"
**SUPER:** *"I clicked buy… but it's not available."*

## SCENE 6 — CLOSE · Back home
**(2:25 – 2:40)**

**VISUAL:** Back to the couch. Daughter holds up her phone to the presenter — the creator's look, now with a clean "Add to bag" inside the video.

**DAUGHTER:** "Dad — can I get this one?"

**PRESENTER (to her, then to camera):**
"She's already shopping from creators. The only real question is whether she can buy it… **from you.**"

**SUPER / END CARD:**
**Tolstoy Influence** — every creator video, a shoppable, inventory-verified storefront.
**94% AI match accuracy.** → *Book a demo*

**(FADE OUT)**

- **Cutdowns:**

  - **:30 social teaser** — Scenes 1 + 5 + end card.
