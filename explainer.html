🧠 How It Works

Three-layer AI pipeline

From raw influencer content to shoppable commerce in seconds.

1

Smart Curation

AI identifies relevant influencers and filters only content featuring apparel and accessories — learning the brand's aesthetic over time.

2

AI Detection & SKU Match

Computer vision scans every frame. OCR extracts brand labels. Products are matched against live inventory in real-time.

3

Human-in-the-Loop

Your team reviews AI-flagged videos before they go live. Only products that are in stock appear on your storefront.

✅ STRICT — Exact Brand + Category + Color + Size ⚡ SOFT — Brand + Category + Color (size differs) ❌ FAIL — Not in inventory → video excluded
