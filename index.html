How It Works

Tolstoy's AI analyzes every influencer video, detects every clothing item, and matches it against your live inventory — so customers only see products they can actually buy.

1
LAYER ONE

Smart Curation

AI identifies relevant influencers and filters only content featuring apparel and accessories — no generic lifestyle videos. Only fashion-forward content makes it through.

2
LAYER TWO

AI Product Detection & SKU Matching

Computer vision scans every frame to detect clothing items and accessories. Each product is matched against your catalog using three precision levels:

STRICT MATCH Brand + Category + Color + Size
SOFT MATCH Brand + Category + Color (different size available)
FAIL Product not in inventory — video is excluded
3
LAYER THREE

Human-in-the-Loop Approval

Videos scoring above 70% confidence are reviewed by your team before going live. Every product shown is verified — if it doesn't match your inventory down to the color, it doesn't appear.

Real-World Example
Video shows: Acne Studios — White T‑Shirt, Size M
✅ Your catalog has it — Shown to customers as shoppable
❌ Only Acne Studios Gray T‑Shirt in stock — Not shown (wrong color, no match)
Expected rejection rate: 15–20% by design — accuracy over volume.
